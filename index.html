fubo.tv/link6 - Enter Activation Code



fubo.tv/link6 is the official activation URL used to connect your FuboTV account to devices like Smart TVs, Roku, Firestick, Apple TV, or gaming consoles. To start streaming, you’ll need to enter the activation code displayed on your TV into a web browser at the provided link.


How to Activate FuboTV on Your Device?

Follow these steps to activate FuboTV quickly:

Step 1: Open the App

  1. Install and open the FuboTV app on your Smart TV or streaming device.

  2. A unique activation code will appear on your TV screen.

Step 2: Go to fubo.tv/link6

  1. On your computer or mobile browser, type fubo.tv/link6 and hit Enter.

  2. You’ll be redirected to the activation page.

Step 3: Enter the Code

  1. Enter the activation code shown on your TV screen.

  2. Sign in with your FuboTV account credentials.

  3. Once confirmed, your TV app will refresh, and you can start streaming.
